hello there

I am an Austin-based information professional currently pursuing a master's degree in Information Studies at the University of Texas School of Information.

My passion is information organization, and I consider myself to be an all-round data wrangler. For the past few years, I've been working with metadata in academic libraries but recently made the transition to industry. I improve product classification and content strategy as a taxonomist for eCommerce startup Edgecase.

Curious about what a taxonomist does? No, we don't stuff animals: that's a taxidermist! In short, taxonomists arrange data thoughtfully, but for a more detailed description, read this short but illuminating blog post published by Earley Information Science: "Taxonomy vs Taxonomy vs Taxonomy".
