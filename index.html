Curious about what a taxonomist does? No, we don't stuff animals: that's a taxidermist! In short, taxonomists arrange data thoughtfully, but if you want a more detailed description, Earley Information Science has published a great blog post on the topic: "Taxonomy vs Taxonomy vs Taxonomy".
